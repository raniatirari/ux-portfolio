About

Design is about
people, not pixels.

I'm a Product Designer and UX Strategist focused on turning complex challenges into intuitive experiences, scalable systems, and meaningful business outcomes. My work spans media, healthcare, events, education, and wellness, leading initiatives from early-stage concepts and platform strategy through research, design, and growth.

I thrive in ambiguity, partnering with product, engineering, and business teams to align vision, uncover opportunities, and bring clarity to complex problems. I believe the best solutions are thoughtful, human-centered, and grounded in real user needs.

What makes my perspective unique is a background that extends beyond design into marketing, sales, and customer-facing roles. This experience gives me a deeper understanding of how products are discovered, adopted, and sustained.

Outside of design, you'll find me teaching Pilates, mentoring emerging designers, or exploring the connection between movement, behavior, and human performance.

Work Process

How I operate.

A glimpse into my collaborative and iterative design process.

01.
Phase One
Discovery Session

Deep user research, stakeholder interviews, and competitive analysis. No assumptions — only evidence and observed behaviour.

02.
Phase Two
Strategy Mapping

Frame the right problem. Map journeys, define success metrics, and align every stakeholder before a single wireframe is drawn.

03.
Phase Three
Prototype Creation

High-fidelity prototypes validated through usability testing. Iterate until intuitive and precise — evidence always wins over opinion.

04.
Phase Four
Final Delivery

Close collaboration through engineering handoff, then monitoring metrics post-launch to verify impact. Proven, not assumed.
